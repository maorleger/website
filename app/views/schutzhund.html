Schutzhund

Schutzhund is a dog sport originally created as a breed suitability test for working German Shepherd Dogs.

You can read more information about it on Wikipedia.
I've been involved in the sport for 3 years now, having achieved the highest Schutzhund title (SchH3) with my first German Shepherd Dog Einstein

I created this page specifically so I can show off my beautiful obedient dog :) So feel free to browse around. There are pictures and some YouTube videos of us working in this amazing sport!
